Rice Planting Test Bed

Undergraduate Research Project

This was a group research project which involved designing and manufacturing a rice planting test bed. As the test bed moved the length of the table, a rack and pinion mechanism actuated the movement of the linkages to plant the rice seedlings. The goal of this project served to test different end effectors prototypes for planting rice seedlings.
